Article Summary

Full Content

Philippine Christian University - Dasmariñas is proud to announce a significant milestone in our digital transformation journey. The PCU-DASMA Connect platform has successfully registered over 1,000 alumni members, representing the largest digital alumni community in the university's history.

This achievement reflects our commitment to maintaining strong connections with our graduates and fostering a vibrant alumni network that spans generations. The platform serves as a bridge between current students, recent graduates, and seasoned professionals who have made their mark in various industries.

Since its launch earlier this year, PCU-DASMA Connect has facilitated numerous connections between alumni and current students, leading to mentorship opportunities, internship placements, and valuable career guidance. The platform has also enabled alumni to stay updated with university news, participate in events, and contribute to the institution's continued growth.

Key Platform Features

  • Comprehensive alumni directory with professional profiles
  • Job opportunity postings from partner organizations
  • Event management and registration system
  • News and announcements from the university
  • Mentorship program facilitation
  • Partnership opportunities with external organizations

"This milestone represents more than just numbers," said Dr. Maria Santos, Director of Alumni Relations. "Each registration signifies a renewed commitment to our alma mater and a willingness to give back to the community that helped shape their professional journey."

The platform continues to grow as more alumni discover its benefits. Recent graduates have particularly embraced the job placement features, while seasoned professionals have actively participated in mentorship programs and guest speaking opportunities.

Looking ahead, PCU-DASMA plans to enhance the platform with additional features including professional development courses, industry-specific networking groups, and expanded partnership opportunities with leading organizations across the Philippines.
