Event Details

The Great Dasmarinas Bay Clean-up Drive is a community event organized in partnership with Clean Seas Alliance to promote environmental stewardship.

Event Objectives

This initiative aims to achieve the following key objectives:

  • Clean 2km of coastline
  • Collect and properly dispose of marine waste
  • Raise awareness about environmental conservation
  • Foster community partnerships
  • Engage PCU-DASMA students in service learning

Schedule

The event will follow this timeline:

  • 8:00 AM - Registration and briefing
  • 9:00 AM - Clean-up activities
  • 12:00 PM - Lunch break
  • 1:00 PM - Educational workshop
  • 3:00 PM - Closing and recognition

Preparation and Requirements

Volunteers are requested to bring sun protection, water bottles, and comfortable clothing. All cleaning materials will be provided on-site.

Expected Impact

The event is expected to remove several tons of waste from the bay area, educate participants on marine conservation, and strengthen university-community partnerships for future initiatives.
