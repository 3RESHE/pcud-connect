About PCU-DASMA Connect

A comprehensive digital platform designed to strengthen relationships between Philippine Christian University - Dasmariñas, its alumni, and external partners.

Our Mission

PCU-DASMA Connect serves as a centralized platform that enables real-time updates, professional networking, event coordination, and collaboration between the university and its external partners.

  • Enhanced alumni engagement and networking
  • Streamlined partnership management
  • Verified job opportunities for alumni
  • Real-time university updates and events
