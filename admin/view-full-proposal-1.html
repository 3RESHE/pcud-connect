Partnership Proposal Overview

DigitalBridge Marketing Solutions proposes a comprehensive 6-week digital marketing workshop series designed to equip PCU-DASMA Business students with cutting-edge digital marketing skills and practical industry experience.

This workshop series covers the complete digital marketing ecosystem including SEO, social media marketing, content strategy, email marketing, paid advertising, and analytics. Students will work on real client projects and earn industry-recognized certifications from Google and HubSpot.

The program will be conducted by certified digital marketing professionals and successful entrepreneurs, ensuring students receive current industry knowledge that directly enhances their career prospects in the rapidly growing digital marketing field.

Program Structure & Curriculum

Week 1-2: Digital Marketing Fundamentals & SEO

  • • Digital marketing landscape and career opportunities
  • • Search Engine Optimization (SEO) fundamentals
  • • Keyword research and content optimization
  • • Hands-on: Building SEO-optimized websites

Week 3-4: Social Media & Content Marketing

  • • Social media platform strategies (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok)
  • • Content creation and visual design principles
  • • Influencer marketing and community building
  • • Hands-on: Creating complete social media campaigns

Week 5: Email Marketing & Automation

  • • Email marketing strategy and best practices
  • • Marketing automation and customer journey mapping
  • • Lead generation and nurturing techniques
  • • Hands-on: Building automated email sequences

Week 6: Analytics & Client Project Presentation

  • • Google Analytics and conversion tracking
  • • ROI measurement and campaign optimization
  • • Client presentation skills and reporting
  • • Final project: Complete digital marketing strategy presentation
