Mission Overview

Free medical consultation, basic health screening, and medicine distribution for underserved communities. No appointment needed - first-come, first-served basis. Includes dental checkup and health education sessions. The mission aims to provide accessible healthcare services to rural communities and promote preventive health awareness.
