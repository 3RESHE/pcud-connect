Organization Background

Literacy Champions Foundation has been promoting literacy and education access in rural communities for 6 years. We have distributed over 15,000 books and established 12 community reading centers. Our programs focus on sustainable literacy development and community engagement through culturally relevant reading materials.

Activity Details

Distribution Plan

• Age-appropriate categorization: Children (0-12), Young Adult (13-18), Adult (18+)
• Mobile distribution units visiting 8 remote barangays
• Storytelling sessions and reading activities at each location
• Establishment of mini-libraries with donated book collections
• Training for local volunteer librarians and reading facilitators

Community Impact Goals

Target distribution of 2,000 books to families in 8 remote barangays. Expected to directly benefit 500+ families and establish sustainable reading programs in each community. Long-term goal is to increase literacy rates and create lasting reading culture in underserved areas.
