Final Results Summary

Highly successful coastal clean-up that exceeded all participation and impact targets. Covered 1.8km of Dasmarinas coastline with 450 community volunteers participating. Achieved 98% recycling rate and generated significant media coverage, strengthening PCU-DASMA's environmental advocacy profile. The event created lasting environmental awareness in the community and established foundations for future environmental partnerships.
