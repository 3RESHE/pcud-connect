By: Communications Staff

The recent back-to-school supplies drive partnership successfully distributed school supplies to 750 students across 12 partner schools in remote barangays, valued at ₱420,000. Strong campus community participation made this initiative highly successful.

Article Content

The Back-to-School Supplies Drive 2025 concluded on September 28, 2025, successfully supporting educational access for students in remote barangays of Dasmarinas and surrounding areas.

Distribution Impact

This comprehensive initiative achieved remarkable results in supporting educational equity:

  • 750 students received complete school supply sets
  • 12 partner schools in remote barangays reached
  • 200 school uniforms distributed to students in need
  • ₱420,000 total value of educational supplies provided
  • 100% target achievement in reaching planned beneficiaries

Community Collaboration

The success of this program demonstrates the power of coordinated community effort. The Education for All Coalition provided funding and supply procurement, while PCU-DASMA organized student volunteers for packing and distribution logistics.

"Seeing the joy on students' faces when they received their supplies reminded us why education access matters. These partnerships create real change in our community." — Ms. Carmen Dela Rosa, Program Coordinator, Education for All Coalition

Student Volunteer Experience

Over 50 PCU-DASMA students participated in the supply packing and distribution activities, gaining valuable experience in community service and understanding educational challenges in rural areas. Many volunteers expressed interest in continuing their involvement in similar initiatives.

Sustainable Impact

Beyond immediate supply distribution, this initiative has strengthened relationships between PCU-DASMA and rural schools, laying groundwork for future educational support programs and mentorship opportunities. The partnership model established this year will serve as a template for expanding educational equity initiatives.
