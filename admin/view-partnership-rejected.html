Organization Background

Community Helpers Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting vulnerable populations in Dasmari単as, Cavite, since 2015. Our mission is to promote food security and community engagement through sustainable outreach programs.

Activity Description

The Weekend Feeding Program aims to provide nutritious meals to street children in Dasmari単as, Cavite. Held every Saturday, the program will serve hot meals to approximately 100-150 children, encouraging open participation from the community. Volunteers will distribute food, engage with participants, and promote social inclusion.

Activity Objectives

  • Provide nutritious meals to at least 100 street children weekly
  • Promote community engagement through volunteer opportunities
  • Raise awareness about food insecurity in the local area

Expected Community Impact

This program will address food insecurity for street children, foster community unity, and encourage volunteerism, creating a positive impact on both participants and the broader Dasmari単as community.
