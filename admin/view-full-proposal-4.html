Research Initiative Overview

The GreenTech Research Institute proposes a comprehensive 2-year research collaboration focusing on cutting-edge sustainable energy technologies. This initiative will establish PCU-DASMA as a leading institution in renewable energy research while contributing to the Philippines' clean energy transition goals.

The research program encompasses three main areas: advanced solar photovoltaic systems, next-generation battery energy storage technologies, and smart grid integration solutions. This multidisciplinary approach will involve faculty members from Engineering, Environmental Science, and Information Technology departments.

Beyond academic research, this initiative aims to develop commercially viable technologies that can be deployed in the Philippine energy sector, creating potential licensing opportunities and startup ventures for participating students and faculty.

Research Focus Areas & Objectives

Solar Energy Systems Research

  • • Development of high-efficiency perovskite-silicon tandem solar cells
  • • Optimization of solar panel performance in tropical climate conditions
  • • Investigation of bifacial solar panel applications for Philippine settings
  • • Cost-effective solar tracking system designs for small-scale installations

Battery & Energy Storage Technologies

  • • Lithium-ion battery optimization for grid-scale energy storage
  • • Research on alternative battery chemistries using local materials
  • • Battery management system development for renewable integration
  • • Life cycle analysis and recycling strategies for energy storage systems

Smart Grid & Integration Solutions

  • • Microgrids design for remote and island communities
  • • Advanced energy management algorithms and control systems
  • • Grid stability analysis with high renewable energy penetration
  • • IoT-enabled monitoring and predictive maintenance systems

Environmental Impact & Sustainability

  • • Life cycle assessment of renewable energy technologies
  • • Environmental monitoring of renewable energy installations
  • • Carbon footprint reduction strategies for energy systems
  • • Economic feasibility studies for sustainable energy adoption
