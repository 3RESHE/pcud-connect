Activity Description

Regular feeding program providing nutritious meals to street children in Dasmarinas area. No registration required - walk-in participation encouraged. Includes basic health check-ups and educational activities. The program aims to provide consistent nutritional support while creating a safe space for street children to access basic services and community connection.

Organization Background

Hope Foundation Inc. has been serving vulnerable communities in Cavite for over 8 years. We specialize in child welfare programs, community feeding initiatives, and educational support services. Our team has successfully implemented 25+ community programs with consistent positive outcomes and strong community partnerships.

Activity Details

Activity Objectives

• Provide regular nutritious meals to street children in Dasmarinas
• Create a safe space for children to access basic health services
• Offer educational activities and literacy support
• Build community awareness about child welfare and protection
• Establish sustainable feeding program with community involvement

Expected Community Impact

This program will directly impact 150 street children per session, providing them with nutritious meals and basic health monitoring. Expected to serve 600 children per month, improving their nutritional status and creating pathways to additional social services. The program will also engage 20-25 PCU-DASMA student volunteers, providing them with meaningful community service experience.
