PCU-DASMA Connect Reaches 1,000+ Alumni Members

Featured
University Update Alumni Platform News

Article Summary

We're excited to announce that PCU-DASMA Connect has successfully onboarded over 1,000 alumni members, creating the largest digital alumni network in the university's history. This milestone represents a significant achievement in fostering stronger connections between our institution and its graduates.

Featured Image

Full Article Content

Today marks a historic milestone for PCU-DASMA as our alumni networking platform, PCU-DASMA Connect, officially welcomes its 1,000th member. This achievement represents more than just a numberâ€”it symbolizes the strength of our alumni community and our commitment to maintaining lifelong connections with our graduates.

Building Bridges

Launched in early 2024, PCU-DASMA Connect was designed to bridge the gap between the university and its alumni, providing a platform for networking, mentorship, job opportunities, and collaborative partnerships. The platform has facilitated over 200 job placements, connected 350 mentors with current students, and enabled countless professional collaborations.

Features and Benefits

The platform offers a comprehensive suite of features including job postings, event management, news updates, partnership opportunities, and direct messaging. Alumni can update their profiles, share their achievements, and stay connected with their alma mater. Current students benefit from mentorship programs and access to a vast network of professionals in various industries.

Looking Forward

"Reaching 1,000 members is just the beginning," says Dr. Robert Cruz, Director of Alumni Relations. "We're continuously enhancing the platform with new features and expanding our reach to include alumni from all years and programs. Our goal is to create the most engaged and supportive alumni community in the region."

We invite all PCU-DASMA graduates to join this growing community and be part of this exciting journey. Together, we're building a network that extends far beyond graduation day.
