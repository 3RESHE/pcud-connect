Published Article Content

PCU-DASMA Connect has reached a significant milestone with the registration of its 1,000th alumni member, marking a new era in digital alumni engagement and community building.

Network Growth and Engagement

Since its launch 18 months ago, the platform has experienced steady growth in alumni participation:

  • 1,000+ registered alumni from classes spanning 1995-2025
  • 85% active users engaging monthly with platform features
  • 150+ job postings shared within the alumni network
  • 75+ mentorship connections established between alumni
  • 50+ successful business partnerships formed through the platform

Platform Impact

The digital platform has transformed how PCU-DASMA alumni stay connected with their alma mater and each other. Key features driving engagement include professional networking tools, mentorship programs, and career development resources.

"PCU-DASMA Connect has been instrumental in advancing my career. The mentorship program connected me with industry leaders, and the job board helped me find opportunities I wouldn't have known about otherwise." — Maria Santos, Class of 2020, Software Engineer

Future Expansion

With this milestone achieved, PCU-DASMA Connect is expanding its features to include alumni event management, fundraising capabilities, and enhanced networking tools. The platform aims to reach 2,000 active alumni members by the end of 2026.

This achievement reflects the strong bonds within the PCU-DASMA community and demonstrates the university's commitment to maintaining lifelong connections with its graduates. The platform continues to evolve based on alumni feedback and emerging technological capabilities.
