Hackathon Event Overview

"Code for Change" is a 48-hour healthcare innovation hackathon designed to bring together students, healthcare professionals, and technology experts to develop practical solutions for pressing healthcare challenges in the Philippines, particularly focusing on healthcare accessibility in rural and underserved communities.

The event aims to foster innovation at the intersection of technology and healthcare, encouraging participants to develop solutions for telemedicine, medical data management, patient monitoring systems, and healthcare delivery optimization. Teams will have access to real healthcare datasets (anonymized) and guidance from medical professionals.

Beyond competition, this hackathon serves as a platform for networking, learning, and potential startup formation, with winning teams receiving not just monetary prizes but also ongoing mentorship and incubation support.

Challenge Tracks & Problem Statements

Track 1: Telemedicine & Remote Healthcare

  • • Develop platforms for remote patient consultation and monitoring
  • • Create solutions for medication adherence in remote areas
  • • Design emergency response systems for isolated communities
  • • Build diagnostic support tools for rural healthcare workers

Track 2: Healthcare Data Management & Analytics

  • • Develop secure electronic health record systems
  • • Create predictive analytics for disease outbreak prevention
  • • Build patient data visualization tools for better decision-making
  • • Design interoperable systems for healthcare data sharing

Track 3: Digital Health & Wellness

  • • Create mental health support applications and platforms
  • • Develop fitness and wellness tracking solutions
  • • Build preventive healthcare and health education tools
  • • Design accessibility solutions for patients with disabilities

Track 4: Healthcare Operations & Logistics

  • • Optimize hospital resource allocation and scheduling systems
  • • Develop supply chain management for medical supplies
  • • Create patient flow and queue management solutions
  • • Build cost optimization tools for healthcare facilities
