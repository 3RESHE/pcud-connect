PCU-DASMA Connect Reaches 1,000+ Alumni Members

Communications Staff

PCU-DASMA Communications Office

1,234 views
89 likes
45 comments

We're excited to announce that PCU-DASMA Connect has successfully onboarded over 1,000 alumni members, creating the largest digital alumni network in the university's history. This milestone represents a significant step in strengthening our alumni community and fostering lifelong connections.

Network Growth and Engagement

Since its launch 18 months ago, the platform has experienced steady growth in alumni participation. The milestone of 1,000 members was achieved through a combination of targeted outreach efforts, word-of-mouth referrals, and the platform's growing reputation for connecting alumni with meaningful opportunities.

Key Platform Statistics

  • 1,000+ registered alumni from classes spanning 1995-2025
  • 85% active users engaging monthly with platform features
  • 150+ job postings shared within the alumni network
  • 75+ mentorship connections established between alumni
  • 50+ successful business partnerships formed through the platform

Platform Impact and Success Stories

The digital platform has transformed how PCU-DASMA alumni stay connected with their alma mater and each other. Key features driving engagement include professional networking tools, mentorship programs, and career development resources.

"PCU-DASMA Connect has been instrumental in advancing my career. The mentorship program connected me with industry leaders, and the job board helped me find opportunities I wouldn't have known about otherwise. It's amazing to see how our alumni community supports each other."
— Maria Santos, Class of 2020, Software Engineer at Tech Innovations Inc.

The platform's success can be attributed to its focus on meaningful connections rather than just networking for the sake of networking. Alumni have reported finding not only job opportunities but also business partners, mentors, and lifelong friends through the platform.

Future Expansion and Development

With this milestone achieved, PCU-DASMA Connect is expanding its features to include alumni event management, fundraising capabilities, and enhanced networking tools. The development team is working on implementing artificial intelligence to improve member matching and recommendation systems.

Upcoming Features (Q1 2026)

  • AI-Powered Networking: Intelligent matching based on interests, career goals, and expertise
  • Virtual Events Platform: Host and attend alumni events online with interactive features
  • Alumni Giving Portal: Streamlined platform for supporting university initiatives
  • Mobile Application: Native iOS and Android apps for enhanced accessibility

The platform aims to reach 2,000 active alumni members by the end of 2026, with a focus on engaging recent graduates and encouraging participation from alumni in international locations.

Recognition and Awards

PCU-DASMA Connect has received recognition from the Philippine Association of Colleges and Universities (PACU) for excellence in alumni engagement. The platform was also featured as a case study in the International Journal of Alumni Relations for its innovative approach to digital community building.

How to Get Involved

Are you a PCU-DASMA alumnus who hasn't joined our community yet? It's never too late to connect with your fellow graduates!

  • • Visit our registration page and create your profile
  • • Update your professional information and interests
  • • Connect with classmates and colleagues
  • • Explore mentorship and career opportunities
  • • Participate in alumni events and activities

This achievement reflects the strong bonds within the PCU-DASMA community and demonstrates the university's commitment to maintaining lifelong connections with its graduates. The platform continues to evolve based on alumni feedback and emerging technological capabilities, ensuring it remains a valuable resource for our growing community.
