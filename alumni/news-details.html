PCU-DASMA Connect Reaches 1,000+ Alumni Members

University Update October 17, 2025 â€¢ By Maria Garcia

We're thrilled to announce that PCU-DASMA Connect has successfully onboarded over 1,000 alumni members, marking a significant milestone in creating the largest digital alumni network in the university's history. This achievement reflects the strong sense of community and engagement among our alumni, who are actively connecting, sharing opportunities, and supporting each other through our platform.

Since its launch, PCU-DASMA Connect has provided a space for alumni to stay updated on university news, participate in events, and access exclusive job opportunities. The platform has facilitated meaningful connections, with over 500 job applications submitted through our job board and 300 alumni attending our recent virtual networking event.

A Growing Community

The rapid growth of our alumni network is a testament to the dedication of our community. From organizing community outreach programs to celebrating alumni achievements, PCU-DASMA Connect continues to foster a vibrant and supportive environment. Our recent partnership with Clean Seas Alliance for the Dasmarinas Bay Clean-up Drive saw 450 volunteers come together, showcasing the power of collective action.

We are also excited to highlight the contributions of our alumni, such as Anna Marie Santos, who recently won the International Young Innovator Award for her groundbreaking work in sustainable technology. Stories like these inspire us to continue building a platform that empowers our alumni to achieve greatness.

What's Next?

As we move forward, PCU-DASMA Connect is committed to enhancing the platform with new features, including a mentorship program, expanded job listings, and more interactive events. We invite all alumni to join us in this journey, share their stories, and contribute to the growth of our community.

Thank you to every member of the PCU-DASMA Connect community for making this milestone possible. Together, we are building a stronger, more connected future for our alumni and university.
